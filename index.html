About

My first build, after months of lurking on SeikoMods. A proper franken: submariner dial, Black Bay bezel, NH35 movement — assembled by hand, one very fiddly part at a time.

The seconds hand nearly broke me. So did the dial feet. But that's the point, isn't it.

On a leather NATO for formal occasions. Marine nationale as the daily driver.
