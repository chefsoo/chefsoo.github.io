Garmin
NFC.

After a 12-year hiatus, I started running again in November 2025. Joined the run team at my gym (shout out Coach Jon) and quickly landed on a simple goal: minimize what I carry on solo runs.

The primary investment was a Garmin Forerunner 255 Music, an older model with a MiP display that handles both tracking and playback. My building entry runs on a passcode, so keys were already out of the picture. The remaining friction: my fob for other access points.

The fix was a 10mm NFC sticker embedded into a nylon watch strap, letting me scan in with a wrist tap. Clean, minimal, no extra carry. NFC has turned out to be a rabbit hole worth going down, and this is just the first project out of it.
